Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate your account and display your personal expense records.
  • To generate summaries and category breakdowns shown in the app.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all associated personal data and expense records are permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements. These providers do not have independent access to your data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via the details provided on our website. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
